Finance Software for Startups

  • QuickBooks

    QuickBooks is an accounting software that allows you to track income and expenses, create invoices, and manage bills. It also integrates with many other financial software tools.

  • Xero

    Xero is an accounting software that is similar to QuickBooks, but with a greater focus on automation and real-time data.

  • Gusto

    Gusto is a payroll and HR software that can automate your employee onboarding process, handle tax compliance, and manage benefits.

  • Expensify

    Expensify is a software for expense tracking and reporting that can help streamline the reimbursement process for employees.

  • FreshBooks

    FreshBooks is an accounting software designed specifically for small business owners that can help with invoicing, time tracking, and project management.

  • NetSuite

    NetSuite is a cloud-based software suite that includes financial management, inventory management, and CRM tools.
